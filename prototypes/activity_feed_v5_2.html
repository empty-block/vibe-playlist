Radical Subtlety Approach

Source Badge Evolution:

  • Positioned as tiny overlay on thumbnail corner
  • 7px monospace font, lowercase only
  • 60% opacity, #606060 color on dark background
  • Almost invisible unless you're looking for it
  • Experimental: Inline after artist or below artist

Outlined Play Button:

  • Cyberpunk-inspired: thin border, no fill
  • Matrix green (#00ff41) for retro-tech aesthetic
  • Hover adds subtle glow without being excessive
  • Active state inverts: green fill, black text
  • Modern-retro fusion that feels fresh

Layout Philosophy:

  • Windows 95 structure with radical minimalism
  • Less visual noise than v5 or v5.1
  • Information hierarchy through subtraction
  • Source badges are metadata, not primary info

Three Badge Approaches Shown:

  1. Corner overlay - Main cards (most subtle)
  2. Inline after artist - Experiment 1 (bullet separator)
  3. Below artist - Experiment 2 (separate tiny line)

The goal: Make source information available but never intrusive. Users who care can find it; users who don't won't be distracted by it.
